0–49 , generally negative

A score of 50 is neutral — your text doesn’t lean strongly positive or negative.

Entity Count & Entities
Entities are key names, places, or things mentioned in your text. If none are detected, it will show 0 and an empty list ().

Repetition Multiplier
This measures repeated ideas across your entries using a glass-box method:

  • Vector Embeddings: Each entry is converted into a numeric vector that captures its semantic meaning.
  • Cosine Similarity: The similarity between your current entry and previous entries is computed as a score from 0 (completely different) to 1 (identical in meaning).
  • Sentiment Adjustment: Positive entries are slightly weighted higher, negative entries slightly lower.
  • Multiplier Calculation: Scores are multiplied by previous entry multipliers to get the new repetition multiplier. Values slightly above 1.0 indicate mild similarity.
  • Interpretation: 1.0 , no repetition detected. >1.0 , mild semantic overlap. Higher numbers indicate stronger repetition patterns.
