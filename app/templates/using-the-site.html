Using The Site

Contributing an Entry


When you create an Entry on PeriDocs.org, we allow you to select whether you'd like your Entry to be saved permanently to our database or only used a temporary search, similar to how a person uses traditional search engines. Where PeriDocs differs is that you are allowed to submit your Entry in any amount of detail that you'd like; we aim to provide search functionality for any Entry that abides by our Terms of Service, but please note that our search capabilities grows stronger with each Entry that you allow us to store as its own persistent post.
