2. Description of Service

PeriDocs is an educational platform focused on heuristic-based semantic analysis, heuristic-based pragmatic interpretation, relevant hyperlink retreival based on the contents of the queries ("Entry," or "Entries") submitted to the platform. The Service allows Users to submit said quieries for educational, reflective, and analytical purposes. Insights are generated for personal learning and reflection only and are not a substitute for professional advice.



  • Entry – a piece of text a user submits to PeriDocs
  • Embedding – a numeric generalization of text meaning
  • Centroid – a group of similar Entries given an overall generalized label assigned by a human
  • Derived Analytical Data – information computed from Entries (such as embeddings, similarity scores, or which Centroids an Entry may be most similar to)
  • Sensitive Personal Information – data revealing things such as race, religion, political opinion, health, or sexual orientation
  • Pseudonymized – data modified so it can’t be directly linked to a person without extra info
  • Audit Trail – a log showing what was done to data and when
